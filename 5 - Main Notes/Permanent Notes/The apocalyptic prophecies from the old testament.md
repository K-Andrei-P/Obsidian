# The apocalyptic prophecies from the old testament

Apocalyptic prophecies from the old testament were told by prophets (messengers of God) like Isaiah. They were build like this: If you don't change this behavior now -> God will do something about it.

This is different from the apocalyptic prophecy of Jesus in the new testament.
